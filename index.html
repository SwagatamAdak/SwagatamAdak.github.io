About Me

I am an innovative and passionate undergraduate Bachelor of Technology student of Computer Science Engineering with a mindset dedicated to streamlining processes and efficiently resolving project issues. I am a quick learner and purpose-driven individual with the belief and determination that a target can be achieved with proper focus, research and execution.

My research experience spans environmental monitoring using multispectral data, cybersecurity threats like Salami attacks, and developing Hybrid Intrusion Detection Systems. More recently, I have been focused on the intersection of AI and hardware design, developing an LLM-based framework for High-Level Synthesis (HLS) at IIT Guwahati. This work involves fine-tuning models for C-to-Verilog correlation and exploring formal verification to simplify and validate complex hardware designs for non-experts.
